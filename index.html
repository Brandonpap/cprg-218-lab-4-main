Street Art

Street art adds a vibrancy into a city, turning blank walls into a captivating canvas. It reflects the community's identity, adding depth and character to urban spaces.

Beyond aesthetics, it fosters creativity and cultural expression, enriching the city's beauty and engaging its citizens.

Calgary's Art Galleries

Illingworth Kerr Gallery

The IKG is a world-class exhibition space and flexible platform for critical enquiry and theoretical debate with a focus on visual culture.

Wallace Galleries Ltd

Ranging from Realism to Abstraction; we carry some of Canada's leading artists, along with some up-and-coming stars.

Glenbow Museum

Art & area history museum with a permanent collection & archives, plus visiting exhibits & events.
